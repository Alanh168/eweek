Build It

Better, Bigger & Beyond

Location: TBA

Build-It it is an annual community outreach event organized and hosted by the University of Miamiâ€™s American Society of Mechanical Engineers. Build-It is primarily a science and engineering based competition where schools get to take part in a timed design and construction effort which they will then put to the test against the other attendees. The winning team is then awarded the Build-It trophy until the next year. Aside from the fun competition Build-It also invites professional speakers to inspire and expose students to the multiple possible and extraordinary routes to success and opportunity in higher education especially in the STEM based fields.
